## SYNOPSIS

```
 [-StartTimeAfter <DateTime>] [-StartTimeBefore <DateTime>] [-TriggerType <String[]>] [-Skip <UInt64>]
 [-First <UInt64>] [-Path <String[]>] [-Recurse] [-Depth <UInt32>] [-ProgressAction <ActionPreference>]
 [<CommonParameters>]
```
